Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how Flappy Cube handles information.

1. Data Collection

Flappy Cube does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

We do not collect information such as:

  • Email addresses or names
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (UDID/IMEI)
  • Contacts or photos

2. How We Use Data

Since we do not collect any data, there is no information to use for account creation, marketing, or tracking purposes. All game progress (high scores) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

3. Third-party Sharing

Flappy Cube does not use any third-party services like Google Analytics, Firebase, or AdMob. Consequently, no data is shared with any third-party entities.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We do not retain any user information on our servers. Because no data is collected, there is no personal information to delete.
