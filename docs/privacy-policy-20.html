Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how Cubey Glide handles information.

1. Data Collection

Cubey Glide collects limited non-personally identifiable information to enhance your gaming experience.

The following data is collected and synchronized:

  • Apple Game Center: Your player name and player identifier (if logged in) for global leaderboards.
  • iCloud Sync: Game progress, including top scores, total games, streaks, max level reached, and unlocked customizations.
  • Firebase: Your Game Center player name and high score are stored in our global leaderboard database.

2. How We Use Data

Data collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Leaderboards: Ranking your performance against other players globally via Game Center and Firebase.
  • Synchronization: Allowing you to keep your game progress and unlocks across all your Apple devices via iCloud.
  • Security: We use Apple's App Attest to ensure the integrity of high score submissions.

3. Third-party Services

Cubey Glide leverages the following third-party services:

  • Apple Inc. (Game Center, iCloud, and App Attest) for synchronization, identity, and security.
  • Google Firebase for hosting our temporary global high score database.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Firebase Leaderboards: Global high scores are stored for 30 days and are automatically deleted thereafter to maintain fresh monthly rankings.

Your Control: You can delete all local, iCloud, and Firebase data at any time via the Manage Data menu in the game settings.
